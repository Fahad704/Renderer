"R" - To toggle ray tracing/Rasterization(Ray tracing not recommanded , only use ray tracing when the poly count is below 100)
"Arrow keys" - to move
"W and S" to move up and down
"Z and X" - to rotate camera left and right
"V" - turn off wireframe view
"B" - to see bounding boxes
"N and M" - to rotate camera up and down(does not work as intended)
"C" - to toggle backface culling
"P" - to export current frame to ppm file
"T" - to turn on wireframe mode
"Q" - to reset position and rotation of camera to 0,0,0
"Space bar" - to view model(s) details
"A" - to toggle anti aliasing (FXAA) (i know i shouldnt have coded it)
"ESC" - to close
